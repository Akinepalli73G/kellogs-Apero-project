What is the promotion?
 
We’re giving you the chance to win a €300 multi-retailer voucher!
How long does the promotion last- what are the start and end dates?
 
The Main Promotional Period starts on the 19th May 2023 and ends on the 30th September for Bosnia & Herzegovina, Albania, Macedonia, Montenegro, Serbia, Kosovo, Slovenia, Bulgaria, Moldova, Slovakia, Estonia & Croatia   The Main Promotional Period starts on the 19th May 2023 and ends on the 15th December 2023 for Romania   You can enter once every day during the Main Promotional Period.
How do I enter?
 
You need to scan the QR code on any Pringles can or on promotional material in store, or you can directly visit Pringles.com. You will then need to either log in to an existing Kellogg’s account or if you are a new user, create a Kellogg’s account. An augmented reality (AR) experience will then start where you will need to complete an AR game in the shortest amount of time possible.
How many times can I enter?
 
You can enter once per day.
How old do I need to be to enter?
 
This promotion is only open to people aged 18 years or older.
Do I need to purchase a can to enter?
 
Yes, you need a Pringles can in order to enter. Participants must: 1. Purchase a can of Pringles; 2. Using a smartphone or tablet, visit Pringles.com and register for a Kellogg’s account If you have not already done so, and complete the multi-factor authentication requirements if required; 3. Launch the AR window on Pringles.com 4. Complete the AR game challenge in the fastest time; 5. Winning entries will be contacted within 28 days via email.
How are winners selected?
 
The entrants who successfully complete the challenge in the quickest time in each Weekly Promotional Period will be deemed the weekly winners for that Weekly Promotional Period.
I can’t log into my account/I have forgotten my password. What should I do?
 
Please follow the ‘Forgotten Password’ or ‘Forgotten Log In Name’ link and follow the instructions on the log in page.
My Pringles can is not being accepted by the augmented reality game, what should I do?
 
Please check you are aligning your Pringles can properly within the AR window, with the Pringles logo on the front. If you are still experiencing problems you can contact us via the Contact Us page at: https://www.pringles.com
Who do I contact if I have any technical issues or questions?
 
You can contact the Kellogg's Careline, via the Contact Us page of our website at: https://www.pringles.com
Who do I contact if I have any technical issues with the prize?
 
For any issues relating to claiming or using your prize please contact our fulfilment partner: promotion@pringles.com
Why am I being asked to provide access to my camera and motion sensors?
 
We need to be able to detect your movement for you to be able to interact with the game. No personal information or footage is being saved during the game.
What browser should I use for the best experience possible?
 
For the best experience possible, we suggest using Safari on iOS or Chrome on Android.
If I win, where should I have my prize sent to?
 
If you are a lucky winner of a multi-retailer voucher, prizes will automatically be sent to the email address you have registered your Kellogg’s account with.
I entered the wrong name/address/email address - can I change it after the order has been placed?
 
We cannot change the details on the order once it has been submitted. Please double check your details before you submit the form.
How long is the multi-retailer voucher valid for?
 
Each multi-retailer voucher code will be issued with a minimum 15-month validity period. If you do not convert the voucher code into a retailer voucher within this time period, you will lose the voucher code value. Each retailer voucher has its own validity period that you can check directly on the voucher provider’s website.
How do I use my multi-retailer voucher?
 
Please follow the steps below to use and redeem your multi-retailer voucher: 1. Winners will be issued a voucher code via email, along with a URL: https://clients.ovationincentives.com/redeem/en/home   2. To redeem, you must visit the URL, and enter your unique voucher code 3. Select the chosen voucher provider including the desired quantity of vouchers for that specific retailer. Once complete add vouchers to the shopping basket. 4. You must then follow the check-out process to confirm your selected voucher.  5. Each voucher is subject to the individual voucher provider’s terms and conditions, which can be found on the voucher provider’s website. Please ensure you review these before selecting your voucher.
